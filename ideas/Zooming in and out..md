# Zooming in and out.
Category theory is a theory of mathematics, but is itself a piece of mathematics, and so it can be used to study itself. We should study relationships between objects, not just the objects themselves. But what about relationships? If we consider those to be new mathematical objects, shouldn’t we study relationships between those? This gives us one more dimension. Why stop there? What about relationships between relationships between relationships?
