# Common sense isn’t common
Why do we need some external advice to follow our common sense? What common sense is? It’s really difficult to stop following common practice because it’s practice and common sense is sense. We tend to be skeptical about senses. That’s why when someone external tells us what we already intuitively know, it’s easier to start following your own common sense. And the best way for an external person to help is to ask questions. People usually don’t challenge the common practice, we blindly follow what we think worked before. By asking questions and challenging our believes we can realise that common practice doesn’t not make any sense.
When you give an advice and especially when you have a proof that it works, it’s very likely to be ignored. The far your advice from what people tend to do the more likely you’ll be ignored. It better to ask questions. It let people find the right answer on their own. So, don’t advice - ask!
