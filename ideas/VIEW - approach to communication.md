# VIEW - approach to communication

VIEW


V - Vulnerability  

I - Impartiality 

E - Empathy 

W - Wander 
  

It describes a state of mind. You don’t need any technics if you’re in this state. 

V - speak your truth even if you’re scary. 

I - not try to achieve a result for you or others

E - be with a person in their emotions

W - the same as curiosity but without a goal.

## V

If you’re pretend to be someone else they will attack someone else. But I declare myself. curage is not a lack of fear, courage is the willingness to feel fear.

## W 
you shouldn’t have a goal because otherwise you can loose potential interesting point that were not planned by you, that’s why it’s not curiosity. It’s not “Do you like ice cream “, it’s “What do you feel when you eat ice cream”. Open questions and open mind. “What makes you such a dick?“ can be asked with judgment and it’s not wander or can be asked with wander, “What happened with you, you behave in a strange way and I want to help/or just wander, it’s interesting, I care ”

Open question gives you more data.

When one has fear they don’t have wander and vice versa. When you push someone to do something you have fear. And this is how we lose things that wander can find. And very often fear is wrong reaction. People trust someone who is in wander and not trust someone who is in fear.

We make emotional decisions and use subconscious because otherwise we spend too much brain power. Who you trust: who wander or who push you to do things.
