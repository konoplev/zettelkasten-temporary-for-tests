# Boarder are restrictive the internals are flexible

The boarders define the protocol of communication with the world. So to protect from errors the boarders should be restrictive. The internals define the behavior, so they should allow to adapt. Make internals as flexible as possible. The less predictable part should be flexible. This is how you can find where the borders are. The most predictable part is the boarders. 
To much control kills internals. To much flexibility kills boarders.
